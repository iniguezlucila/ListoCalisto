1, 2, 3, TAMI


“1, 2, 3 TAMI” is a preschool musical series where Tami, a curious and cheerful huemul, lives small adventures with her friends Beto, Mika, Dana, and Bello Cordero. Every day they face new emotions, like fear, anger, or joy, and transform them into songs that help children recognize and express what they feel. They are always joined by Don Pingüé, Tami’s grandfather, who patiently and lovingly shares his wisdom. Together, the group discovers that growing up is easier and more fun when you learn to feel in good company.
